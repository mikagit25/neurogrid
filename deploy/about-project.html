What is NeuroGrid?

NeuroGrid is a revolutionary decentralized platform that connects idle computing resources worldwide to create a distributed AI inference network. We're building the future where AI computing is accessible, affordable, and democratized.

By allowing users to monetize their GPU resources and providing affordable, scalable AI computing for developers, researchers, and companies, NeuroGrid democratizes access to artificial intelligence.
